The Best Movies According to Giorgi

My top movies of all-time

Gladiator

A former Roman General sets out to exact vengeance against the corrupt emperor,
who murdered his family and sent him into slavery.

The Matrix

When a beautiful stranger leads computer hacker Neo to a forbidding underworld,
he discovers the shocking truth--the life he knows is the elaborate deception of an evil cyber-intelligence.

The Dark Knight

When the menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham,
Batman, James Gordon and Harvey Dent must work together to put an end to the madness.
